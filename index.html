Game Introduction

Beat Monster is a unique rhythm-based action game where you defeat hordes of monsters by hitting the beats in sync with the music. The more accurately you hit the beats, the stronger your attacks become, allowing you to beat down your enemies and progress through challenging levels. Can you become the ultimate monster slayer?
